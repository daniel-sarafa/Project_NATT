Project NATT

Project Natt is a new app that allows users to be rewarded for not using your phone while driving. It's simple, turn on drive mode in the app and gather points to use on rewards such as gift cards. Drive mode will shut off most functionality on your phone to ensure that, while you are gaining points, you are also being a safe driver. Reward yourself for being a safe driver today, and sign up here!
